Calculating Portfolio Variance¶

$$ {Portfolio variance} = w_1^2\sigma_1^2 + w_2^2\sigma_2^2 + 2w_1w_2Cov_{1,2} $$

Where:

$$ w_1 $$

= the portfolio weight of the first asset

$$ w_2 $$

= the portfolio weight of the second asset

$$ \sigma_1 $$

= the standard deviation of the first asset

$$ \sigma_2 $$

= the standard deviation of the second asset
